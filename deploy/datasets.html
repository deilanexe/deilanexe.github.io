Dataset for Semantic Relatedness using Domain Information


The files in the links were used for the experiments described in Chapters 5 and 6 of my thesis, and in the following paper:

Macias-Galindo, D., Cavedon, L., Wong, W., and Thangarajah, J., Effects of Domain on Measures of Semantic Relatedness. Journal of the American Society for Information Science and Technology. Vol. 66, Issue No. 10, ISSN: 2330-1643. Pages 2116-2131.

The three files are stored in CSV format.
Each line corresponds to a pair of concepts (from the same domain or from different domains), as follows:

Same Domain Subset (ssd.csv): concept A,definition A, concept B, definition B, domain, average score
Same Domain Subset with wikilinks (ssd_w.csv): concept A,definition A, concept B, definition B, domain, wikilinks A-to-B, wikilinks B-to-A, average score
Cross Domain Subset (xdd_end.csv):

  • General: concept A,definition A, concept B, definition B, domain A, domain B, average score
  • Wiki-cross domain: concept A,definition A, concept B, definition B, domain A, domain B, wikilinks A-to-B, wikilinks B-to-A, average score
  • Same domain with very similar automatically-assigned scores: concept A,definition A, concept B, definition B, domain, wikilinks A-to-B, wikilinks B-to-A, assigned score, average score
  • Cross domain with very similar automatically-assigned scores: concept A,definition A, concept B, definition B, domain A, domain B, wikilinks A-to-B, wikilinks B-to-A, assigned score, average score
Definitions have been extracted from WordNet 3.0, or from Wikipedia for nearly-similar pairs in the Same Domain Subset.
